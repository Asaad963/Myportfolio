Coffee Shop Branding

Crafting a Welcoming Brand Identity

Overview

This project focuses on product aesthetics and brand identity, showcasing how innovative design can elevate a classic. It delves into visual dynamics and high-performance branding, capturing the raw power and refined elegance of an iconic automotive legend. The goal was to craft a warm and inviting visual identity, including logo design, typography, and atmospheric elements. Designing for a greener future, this project explores sustainable packaging and brand messaging for an eco-conscious product line, emphasizing natural appeal and environmental responsibility.
