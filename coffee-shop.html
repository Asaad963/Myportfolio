Coffee Shop Branding

Crafting a Welcoming Brand Identity

Overview

This project centered on crafting a warm and inviting visual identity for a modern coffee shop, aiming to create a cohesive and memorable brand experience. My role involved developing a unique logo design that reflects the shop's ambiance, selecting a harmonious typography system, and designing atmospheric elements that resonate with coffee lovers. The goal was to translate the comfort and sensory delight of a coffee shop into a compelling visual language, suitable for everything from storefront signage to packaging and digital marketing materials.
